About Us

Hamlet 37 is the name of the store, and it is not a reference to the character Hamlet from Shakespeare. The store was also named after the owner, whose name is Rb. The number 37 is a reference to the periodic table, rubidium, which is represented by the symbol "Rb".

Hamlet 37 is a store that sells a variety of goods, including stickers, button down shirts, phone cases and more with a vintage feel. The store is located in Janagdong, Mogpog and is known for its unique selection of goods. Hamlet 37 is a great place to find gifts for yourself and others. The stickers and button down shirts are popular among fans of the retro aesthetic, and the phone cases come in a variety of styles. If you're in Marinduque and are looking for a unique gift, be sure to check out Hamlet 37.
